Lease negotiation response

123 Collins Street, Melbourne

Tenant: Acme Retail Pty Ltd

Please find our response to the Heads of Agreement for the above premises. We have set out our proposed amendments and the clauses we have accepted below.

Proposed changes · 3
Cl 5.1 Rent Review
Proposed wording
Rent reviews shall be calculated as the lesser of CPI or 4% per annum. Ratchet provisions shall not apply and base rent shall not increase beyond the reviewed amount.
Cl 8 Bank Guarantee
Proposed wording
The bank guarantee amount is to be reduced to 3 months' gross rent ($37,500). The guarantee shall be released within 30 days of lease expiry provided all obligations have been met.
SC 3 Make Good
Proposed wording
Make good obligations are to be limited to removing the tenant's own fixtures and fittings and returning the premises in a clean condition. No structural reinstatement to the original shell condition shall be required.
Accepted as drafted · 4
Cl 2 Lease Term — 5 years with 5-year option
Cl 3 Rent-free Period — 3 months
